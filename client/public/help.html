For Customers

To place an order:

  1. Browse products in our catalog
  2. Add items to your cart
  3. Go to cart and review your items
  4. Click "Checkout" and complete payment
  5. You'll receive an order confirmation

We accept all major payment methods through Razorpay secure gateway:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, RuPay, American Express
  • Net Banking: All major banks supported
  • UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and all UPI apps
  • Digital Wallets: Paytm, Mobikwik, Freecharge
  • EMI Options: Available on select cards
  • International Cards: Accepted with currency conversion

All payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance.

You can track your order by:

  • Going to your Profile → Order History
  • Viewing order status and details
  • Contacting the vendor directly if needed

Our return policy varies by vendor, but generally:

  • Returns accepted within 7-14 days
  • Items must be in original condition
  • Contact the vendor first for return requests
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

To leave a review:

  1. Go to Profile → Order History
  2. Find your completed order
  3. Click "Write Review" next to the product
  4. Rate and write your feedback
  5. Submit your review

For Vendors

To become a vendor:

  1. Register as a vendor on our platform
  2. Complete your store profile
  3. Set up payment details (bank account, PAN, etc.)
  4. Wait for verification (1-3 business days)
  5. Start adding products to your store

Our commission structure is transparent and competitive:

  • Commission Rate: Only 1% of each sale (Industry lowest!)
  • Your Earnings: You receive 99% of the sale amount
  • No Hidden Fees: Zero setup, listing, or monthly charges
  • Fast Settlements: Payments processed within 24-48 hours
  • Real-time Tracking: Monitor earnings in your dashboard
  • Multiple Payout Options: Bank transfer, UPI, or digital wallets
  • Tax Support: GST invoicing and TDS handling included

Example: On a ₹1000 sale, you earn ₹990, we charge only ₹10 as commission.

To add products:

  1. Go to your Vendor Dashboard
  2. Click "Add Product"
  3. Fill in product details (name, price, stock, images)
  4. Set your pricing and inventory
  5. Publish your product

Payment timeline:

  • Order completion: When customer receives the order
  • Processing time: 24-48 hours after completion
  • Settlement: Direct bank transfer to your account
  • Tracking: View earnings in your dashboard

Order management:

  • View all orders in your dashboard
  • Update order status (processing, shipped, delivered)
  • Communicate with customers
  • Handle returns and refunds
  • Track your sales analytics
