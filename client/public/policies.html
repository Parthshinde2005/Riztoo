Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. General Refund Policy

Refunds are handled by individual vendors according to their specific policies. Riztoo facilitates the refund process but does not directly process refunds.

2. Refund Eligibility

  • Items must be returned in original condition
  • Refund requests must be made within the vendor's specified timeframe
  • Perishable items may not be eligible for refunds
  • Custom or personalized items may have different policies

3. Refund Process

  1. Contact the vendor directly through the platform
  2. Provide order details and reason for refund
  3. Follow vendor's return instructions
  4. Vendor processes refund after receiving returned item
  5. Refund credited to original payment method within 5-7 business days

4. Platform Guarantee

If a vendor fails to respond to refund requests or violates our policies, Riztoo may intervene to ensure fair resolution.

5. Dispute Resolution

For unresolved disputes, contact our support team at support@riztoo.com. We will mediate between customers and vendors to reach a fair solution.
